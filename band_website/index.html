Imagine Dragons!

Imagine Dragons is an American pop rock band based in Las Vegas, Nevada, consisting
of lead singer Dan Reynolds, guitarist Wayne Sermon, bassist Ben McKee and drummer Daniel Platzman

They have toured all over the world, delivering their indie-style of pop rock to the continents.

Their second album smoke + mirrors reached the number one spot in the US, Canada, and the UK.
